Some interesting facts about camels:

  • They store excess food and water in the hump on their back.
  • They can live up to months without food or water.
  • Camels in cold deserts grow fur to keep warm.


Some interesting facts about pigeons:

  • Pigeons are very social and intelligent.
  • They have good navigation abilities.
  • They have very good auditory abilities.

Some interesting facts about pandas:

  • There are only a few hundred pandas left in the wild.
  • Pandas are a species of bear.
  • They can sleep upto 10 hours a day.

Some interesting facts about chameleons:

  • They can blend within their sorroundings.
  • They are a species of lizards.
  • They are especially abundunt in the Amazon rainforest.

Some interesting facts about cicadas:

  • They are only awake at night.
  • Some stay underground and come to the surface only every 13-17 years.
  • They are eaten as cuisine in some countries.

Some interesting facts about teddy bears:

  • They are not real animals.
  • The first recorded teddy bear in history was made in 1568.
  • Teddy bears were originally expensive toys only affordable to royalty.
